# NRD Lists – Block Newly Registered Domains

**Status:** Under Maintenance – The repository is currently being rebuilt.

The NRD lists help block newly registered domains (NRDs) that are often used for malicious purposes such as malware distribution. The lists are updated frequently and are compatible with various DNS filtering tools.

---

## Overview

| **Type**       | **Description**                                                                 |
|----------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| NRD 14/30      | Complete lists containing all newly registered domains.                       |
| DGA 14/30      | Lists of domains generated by malware Domain Generation Algorithms (DGAs).    |
| Phish 14/30    | **Experimental:** Domains imitating brands (high false positive rate).        |

**Note:** Some lists are split into parts due to their size and may cause high system memory usage.

**References on NRDs and DGAs:**
- [What are malicious NRDs? – Palo Alto Networks](https://www.paloaltonetworks.com/cyberpedia/what-are-malicious-newly-registered-domains)
- [The danger of DGAs – FS.com](https://community.fs.com/encyclopedia/dga.html)

---

## Available Lists

| **List Name**              | **Description**                                                                 |
|----------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 14-Day NRD                 | Blocks domains registered in the past 14 days.                                |
| 30-Day NRD                 | Blocks domains registered in the past 30 days.                                |
| 14-Day DGA                 | Domains generated by malware DGAs in the past 14 days.                        |
| 30-Day DGA                 | Domains generated by malware DGAs in the past 30 days.                        |
| 14-Day Phishing NRD        | **Beta:** Examining brand-imitating domains from the past 14 days.            |
| 30-Day Phishing NRD        | **Beta:** Examining brand-imitating domains from the past 30 days.            |

---

## Features

| **Feature**                 | **Details**                                                                 |
|------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Block NRDs                   | Prevents access to newly registered domains before they can be used for malware. |
| Multi-Format Support         | Available in Domain-only and Adblock formats for compatibility with various tools. |

---

## Supported Formats

| **Format**         | **Purpose**                                      | **Compatible Tools**                                                                                  |
|--------------------|-------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Domain-only        | Standard domain list for DNS filtering tools.  | DNSCloak, DNSCrypt, PersonalDNSfilter, InviZible Pro                                               |
| Adblock            | Adblock syntax for compatible tools.           | Pi-hole, AdGuard Home, TechnitiumDNS, uBlock, AdBlock, AdBlock Plus, Opera, Vivaldi, Brave, eBlocker, AdNauseam |

---

## List Update Schedule

- Lists are updated at least once every 24 hours at 05:10 UTC.  
- Files are timestamped to reflect the latest update cycle.

---

## Important Notes

- **False Positives:** Legitimate domains may occasionally be blocked. Review and whitelist essential domains.  
- **Phishing Lists:** Experimental; only for examination purposes. High false positive rate expected.  
- **File Size:** Lists are large; insufficient system memory may cause crashes in tools or browsers.
